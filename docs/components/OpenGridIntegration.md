# OpenGrid Integration

<img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridbaseplate.jpg" alt="An OpenGrid baseplate glue-on" width="256" height="256"> <img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridmountbottom.jpg" alt="An OpenGrid mount, bottom view" width="256" height="256">

Plaice integrates with OpenGrid bidirectionally, with the ability to mount OpenGrid baseplates to Plaice plates and vice-versa.
Four Plaice cell units are identical to three OpenGrid cell units, which means that the length required to synchronize
is `84mm`. 

## Mounts
<img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridmountbottom.jpg" alt="An OpenGrid mount, bottom view" width="256" height="256"> <img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridmounttop.jpg" alt="An OpenGrid mount, top view" width="256" height="256">

The "mount" allows connecting a Plaice plate to an OpenGrid baseplate, and it consists of an array of Plaice nubbed connectors
on one side and OpenGrid snaps on the other.

### Printing Tips
<img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridprintability.webp" alt="An OpenGrid mount just after printing" width="256" height="256">

The OpenGrid mount should be printed with supports only added to support the large flat just beyond all the nubbed connectors' chamfers for ease of removal.

## Baseplates
<img src="/docs/images/opengrid/opengridbaseplate.jpg" alt="An OpenGrid baseplate glue-on" width="256" height="256">

The "baseplate" is a glue-on to `4 CU x 4 CU` Plaice clipped covers which adds an OpenGrid baseplate.
