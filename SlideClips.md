# Slide Clips

Plaice slide clips fuse a clipped plug to a protruding slide connector, with positioning set to match the positioning
of plates which would otherwise be obtained via crosses and tees. (TODO: link others) They are largely useful
for adding impromptu "dividers" to box-like Plaice constructions without needing to deconstruct and reconstruct
the larger container, as would be required for crosses and tees. 

Given their currently limited "add-on" functionality, there are at present no other variants of slide clips (to e.g:
have glides/use nubs/ have intruding slide connectors), because the default design here has the best properties
(stability, correct "standard" orientation) for the presently-understood use-cases. 

## Printing
Print with the clip facing upwards and the slide on the bottom, with supports enabled. Optionally block supports
inside of the nut channels for the clips on top.
